Interest
I am a theoretical physicist, working mainly in the field of (but not limited to) high-energy particle and nuclear physics. I am particularly interested in physics under extreme conditions. I would like to understand intriguing physics phenomena occuring there based on the fundamental theory of elementary particles, and, conversely, use such understanding to deepen our knowledge of the "ordinary" nature surrounding us from a miscroscopic point of view. Specifically, I have been working on:
✔Strong-field physics
✔Quantum chromodynamics at high temperature and density
✔Phenomenology of heavy-ion collisions
