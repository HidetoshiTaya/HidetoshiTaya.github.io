EM field in intermediate-energy non-central HIC
GIF animations of the spacetime evolution of the electromagnetic field produced in non-central collisions of gold ions at intermediate energies \( \sqrt{s_{\rm NN}} = O(3-10\;{\rm GeV}) \).

Note that (1) the beam direction is taken along the \( z \)-axis; (2) the collision energy is fixed as \( \sqrt{s_{\rm NN}} = 4.5\;{\rm GeV} \); (3) the event average is already taken; (4) the black dahsed circles are guides, indicating the rough locations of the colliding ions.
