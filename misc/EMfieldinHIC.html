EM field in intermediate-energy central HIC
GIF animations of the spacetime evolution of the electromagnetic field produced in central collisions of gold ions at intermediate collision energies \( \sqrt{s_{\rm NN}} = O(3-10\;{\rm GeV}) \).

Note that the beam direction is taken along the \( z \)-axis. Note also that \( {\mathcal F} := E^2 - B^2 \) is the electromagnetic Lorentz invariant and the magnetic field is almost vanishing at the central events and so \( {\mathcal F} \approx E^2 \).
